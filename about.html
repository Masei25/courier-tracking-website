About Us

Royale Courier Service is a leading logistics and distribution services company established in 2009. We offer a wide array of express courier and logistic support solutions to our various customers. With over 400 highly skilled personnel, we have excellent competence in striving sectors such as Financial Services, Manufacturing, Telecommunication, Government Agencies, and Oil & Gas/Utilities. As a diverse end-to-ed logistics solutions provider, we offer a range of expertise aimed at helping customers re-engineer and re-invent their business to compete successfully in an ever-changing marketplace, with the final objective of safety and promptly delivering all our customers consignments as expected.!

Our Delivery Services

Royale Courier offers a comprehensive range of the express, timed and economy delivery services. Whether you simply want to check the cheapest way to send a parcel or you need an overnight courier and the fastest delivery times.
